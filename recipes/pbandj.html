Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

One of the classics, this recipe is for an all-time favorite: the Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich. Short, sweet, and to the point!

You will find both the ingredient list and steps to create this wonderful dish below.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter (alternatively, crunchy can be used)
  • Jelly (personal preferences as to what kind, grape is a classic)
  • Two slices of bread
  • A means to spread the peanut butter and jelly, typically a butter knife

Steps

  1. Place your two slices of bread on a flat surface
  2. Open both containers of peanut butter and jelly
  3. Use your butter knife, or other tool, to spread either the peanut butter or jelly on to one slice of bread
  4. Use your butter knife, or other tool, to spread the other topping
  5. After both slices of bread are covered, place the peanut butter side and jelly side together to complete your sandwich.
  6. Close the peanut butter and jelly containers with their appropriate lids
  7. Enjoy!
