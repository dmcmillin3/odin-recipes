Chicken and Dumplings

Description

This chicken and dumplings dish is not your average, ordinary, every day dish. This exquisite recipe features ingredients and guesstimations straight from MeMe's kitchen! Seasoned to perfection, you'll find yourself enjoying a bowl of this dish next to a warm fire, snuggled in a blanket, during a cool fall evening; the perfect compliment to a relaxing conclusion to your day.

You will find both the ingredient list and steps to create this wonderful dish below.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chicken broth
  • Biscuit dough
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil some chicken
  2. Start some boiling chicken broth
  3. Drop biscuit dough in broth
  4. Shred chicken
  5. Once biscuit dough is cooked, put shredded chicken in the broth
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
